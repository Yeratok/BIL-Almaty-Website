Who We Are

Built on Excellence
Since 1992

Almaty Bilim-Innovation Lyceum is a state-funded boys' lyceum under the Bilim-Innovation Foundation — one of Kazakhstan's most respected educational institutions, founded to produce the country's future scientists, engineers, and leaders.

We offer trilingual instruction across Kazakh, Russian, and English for grades 7–11, with an on-campus dormitory open to students from all regions of Kazakhstan.
